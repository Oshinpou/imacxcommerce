Refunds & Returns Policy

At Imacx, we strive to provide a luxurious and seamless shopping experience. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, we have a clear and fair Refunds & Returns policy to ensure your convenience and satisfaction.

1. Eligibility for Returns

Products are eligible for return if:

  • They are unused, unopened, and in the same condition as received.
  • Return request is made within 3 days of delivery.
  • The product does not violate hygiene or safety restrictions.

2. How to Request a Return

To initiate a return, please contact our support team via:

  • Email: macxyy12@gmail.com
  • Phone: +91 84529 39172

Provide your order ID and reason for return. Our team will guide you through the return process.

3. Refund Process

Once we receive the returned product and confirm its condition, your refund will be processed as follows:

  • Full refund for products returned in original condition.
  • Refunds will be made to the original payment method used during checkout.
  • The process typically takes 3-5 business days from the date we receive your return.

4. Exchange Policy

Currently, we do not provide direct exchanges. Please return the original item and place a new order for the desired product.

5. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective product:

  • Report it immediately within 3 days of receipt.
  • Include photos of the damage along with your order ID.
  • We will provide a replacement or full refund after verification.

6. Non-Returnable Items

Certain items cannot be returned due to hygiene or safety reasons. These include:

  • Opened cosmetics or skincare products.
  • Personalized or customized products.
  • Gift cards or vouchers.

7. Shipping for Returns

Return shipping is the responsibility of the customer unless the return is due to a defect or mistake by Imacx. Use secure packaging to avoid damage during transit. Imacx is not responsible for items lost during return shipping.

8. Cancellations

You may cancel an order within 3 days of purchase if it has not been shipped. Contact our support immediately to process the cancellation and refund.

9. Contact Support

For any questions, concerns, or assistance regarding refunds and returns, reach out to us at macxyy12@gmail.com or call +91 84529 39172. We aim to respond promptly and resolve your issues efficiently.

This Refunds & Returns policy ensures transparency and a worry-free shopping experience for all Imacx customers. We value your trust and strive to provide a luxurious, safe, and satisfying purchase experience.
